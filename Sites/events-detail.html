The Insurance Authority and Dubai Trade sign a memorandum of understanding to provide insurance services electronically

The campaign covered various areas & issued 670 fines & 300 written warnings to offending facilities for failing to comply with health & environment regulations, in addition to confiscating 3000 kilograms of expired foodstuff. The campaign adopted a specific work mechanism basing on the powers granted to the inspection teams by conducting a series of sudden evening inspection campaigns on daily basis for different supermarkets, cafeterias, restaurants & stores.

Ghanim Ali, Head of Health & Environment Department said: ‘’ Food safety responsibility is not only limited to our department but it’s a shared responsibility for all food facilities from the factory to the consumer in terms of manufacturing, Importing, transporting, storing, distributing, sales points and finally the consumer, thus our goal is to prepare a food safety management system shared by all food chain sectors , which is based on risk assessment principles to find the best solutions to gain the consumer’s confidence.
