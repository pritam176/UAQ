Opening Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Marine Research Center in Umm Al Quwain

Monday, 04 March 2015 16:25

H. H. Sheikh Saud bin Rashid Al Mu'alla, Supreme Council member and ruler of Umm al-Qaiwain today was briefed by Dr. Rashid Ahmed bin Fahad, Minister of Environment and Water, on the Ministry's current and future plans in the emirate.

Sheikh Saud hailed the efforts of the Ministry to provide excellent services and to develop environmental services.

He also was briefed on plans to build the Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Marine Research Center in Umm Al Qaiwain, in the presence of both Engineer Zahra Al Oboody, Acting undersecretary of Ministry of Public work & Mr. Sultan Abdullah Elwan, Assistant Undersecretary of the ministry.

Dr. Rashid said’’ The center will work on feeding natural reserves along the coast of the emirate for protecting and developing fish wealth by conducting studies, research and experiments on native species as well as encouraging and supporting local fish farming’’.

Engineer Zahra Al Oboody, Acting undersecretary of Ministry of Public work said ’’ the research centre well be developed using modern techniques in line with the future plans of the Ministry of Environment and Water, Pointing out that the Ministry of Public Works has prepared the necessary studies and it’s following up the project progress in collaboration with the Ministry of Environment & water in order to achieve the desired objective of this establishment’’.
